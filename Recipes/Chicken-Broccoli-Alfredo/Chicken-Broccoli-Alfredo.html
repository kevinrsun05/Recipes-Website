How to Make Creamy Chicken and BTS Broccoli Alfredo

The Best Pasta you'll ever have (BTS likes it)

Elevate your dinner with the classic Chicken Alfredo, but with a twist. This tasty dish takes the rich Alfredo sauce and adds a nutritious touch of broccoli, leaving your taste buds in heaven and keeping your body healthy. How do you think BTS members can stay so fit and healthy?

Ingredients

  • 8 ounces fettuccine (or any pasta), uncooked
  • 2 cups fresh broccoli florets
  • ¼ cup Kraft Zesty Italian Dressing
  • 1 pound boneless skinless chicken breasts, cut into bite-sized pieces
  • 1⅔ cups milk
  • 4 ounces Philadelphia Cream Cheese, cubed
  • ¼ cup Kraft Grated Parmesan Cheese
  • ½ teaspoon dried basil leaves

Directions

  1. Cook the damn pasta as directed on the package and add broccoli to the boiling water for the last 2 minutes of the pasta cooking time. Drain pasta mixture. There's no fucking way you mess this part up.

  2. Meanwhile (while the pasta is cooking if you can't understand english), heat dressing in large nonstick skillet on medium-high heat (figure out the temperature goof). Add the chicken and cook until it is not pink in the center anymore, stirring occasionally, ~5-7 minutes. REMEMBER THERE IS NO MEDIUM OR RARE FOR CHICKEN. COOK THAT SHIT WELL DONE!

  3. Stir the pasta in milk, cream cheese, Parmesan cheese, and basil. Bring to a boil, stirring constantly. Keep going until the sauce is cooked well (figure it out), ~1-2 minutes.

  4. Add the chicken mixture to the pasta mixture and mix lightly.
  5. Eat your pasta now
